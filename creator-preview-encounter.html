Bandits on the Whisper Road
Forest · Levels 3–3 · Party of 4
Intended Medium
Computed Hard (1100 adj XP)

The wagon track narrows between two moss-hung oaks where the road hollows into a shallow gully. Crows scatter from the trees as the party approaches. Arrows whistle from the underbrush before the first bandit steps into view.

Enemies
  • 4× Bandit (CR 1/8, 25 XP) — Hide in brush, take Hide action first round
  • 1× Bandit Captain (CR 2, 450 XP) — Parleys first if approached peacefully; fights if outnumbered
Terrain
Dense Underbrush. Half the map is difficult terrain granting half cover to anyone crouching. Perception DC 13 to spot bandits within.
Overturned Wagon. Blocks the path. Three-quarters cover behind it. AC 15, 30 HP, immune to poison/psychic.
Tactics

Captain stays near the wagon, using it for cover. Archers focus on lightly armored targets. Once the captain falls below half HP, the remaining bandits make a Wisdom save DC 12 or flee.

Treasure

Captain's pouch: 42 gp, 15 sp, silver locket (25 gp), a potion of healing. Bandits each carry 1d6 sp and common weapons. In the wagon: crate of salted pork, 20 lb; sealed letter bearing a local noble's crest.

Alternatives

DC 15 Persuasion to pay off the captain (100 gp minimum). DC 17 Intimidation to scatter the bandits. DC 13 group Stealth to bypass the ambush entirely via the eastern creek bed.
