Getting Started with D&D

Whether you've never rolled a d20 or you're looking for your first character, this is the place to begin. We'll walk you through everything you need to start playing.

Learn the Basics

Understand how the game works: rolling dice, ability checks, combat, and magic. Our interactive tutorial walks you through each concept step by step with hands-on practice.

2
Pick a Character

Browse our gallery of premade heroes, each with a backstory, ability scores, and equipment ready to go. Find one that fits your playstyle and jump straight into a game.

3
Join an Adventure

Ask a friend who's a Dungeon Master, find a local game store, or join an online community. All you need is your character, some dice, and a willingness to explore.
