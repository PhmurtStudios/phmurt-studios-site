Export my data

We'll build a JSON bundle containing every row tied to your account — your profile, characters, campaigns, collections, and the events/visits/errors that reference your user ID. The file will download straight to your device.

  • Format: JSON (structured, machine-readable)
  • Rate limit: up to 3 exports per hour
  • No other user's personal data is included

Delete my account

This permanently removes your account and all content you own. This cannot be undone.

What happens when you delete:

  • Any active subscription is canceled first (best-effort).
  • Your profile, characters, campaigns (with owned children), collections, and homebrew are permanently deleted.
  • Analytics rows (visits/events/errors) that reference your user ID are anonymized (your user ID is cleared) so aggregate metrics stay intact.
  • Your authentication record is deleted last.
