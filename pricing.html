Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try Pro features before subscribing?
The free tier gives you full access to the character builder, character sheets, dice roller, and basic campaign management so you can get a feel for the platform before upgrading.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — cancel from your account menu at any time. You'll keep Pro access through the end of your billing period, then revert to the free tier. No cancellation fees.
What happens to my characters if I cancel?
Your characters and campaigns are never deleted. On the free tier you can still view and edit your first 3 characters and 1 campaign. The rest stay safe in your account and become accessible again if you re-subscribe.
Is my payment secure?
All payments are handled by Stripe. Phmurt Studios never sees or stores your card details.
Can I switch between monthly and yearly?
Yes — manage your billing cycle from your subscription settings. Switch at any time and we'll prorate the difference.
What happens after 200 Lifetime Passes sell out?
The Lifetime Pass is an early supporter offer limited to the first 200 members. Once sold out, this offer won't return at this price. Lifetime members keep access to every piece of content we ever publish — free and premium — forever. You'll still be able to subscribe to Pro, Party Pass, or other plans after it sells out.
