Stormwarden
Prerequisite: Wisdom 13 or higher, ability to cast at least one spell

You have bound your will to the raging storm, drawing power from tempest and sky. Thunderclaps accompany your steps in battle.

Benefits
Increase your Wisdom score by 1.
  • You gain resistance to lightning and thunder damage.
  • As a bonus action, you can expend a spell slot to deal 2d8 thunder damage (+1d8 per slot above 1st) in a 10-foot radius.
  • You learn the gust cantrip. Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for it.
