School of Pyromancy
Wizard subclass · Fire, Elemental

A school dedicated to the mastery of fire magic. Pyromancers learn to harness the destructive and purifying power of flames, bending them to their will in battle and in the pursuit of knowledge.

Expanded Spells
Level Spells
1st Burning Hands, Hellish Rebuke
2nd Scorching Ray, Flaming Sphere
3rd Fireball, Stinking Cloud
Subclass Features
  • Lv2 Pyromantic Spark: When you cast a fire spell, you can add your Intelligence modifier to the fire damage.
  • Lv6 Inferno Mastery: When you cast a spell that deals fire damage, you can spend a sorcery point to reroll fire damage dice.
  • Lv10 Heart of Flame: You gain resistance to fire damage. When you take fire damage, you can use your reaction to gain immunity to that damage this turn.
  • Lv14 Phoenix Rebirth: When you are reduced to 0 hit points, you can spend a sorcery point to instead gain temporary hit points equal to your wizard level and cast scorching ray as a bonus action.
