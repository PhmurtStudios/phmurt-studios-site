Blade of Forgotten Kings
Magic Weapon · Named Weapon, Very Rare (requires attunement)
Damage 2d6+2 slashing
Properties Finesse, Special, Versatile, Magical
Value 15,000 gp
Weight 3 lb.

A longsword of blackened steel, its pommel set with a dull garnet. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

Once per dawn, you may speak the name of a lost monarch to cause the blade to flare with regal silver fire for one minute, dealing an additional 1d6 radiant damage to any target it strikes.
