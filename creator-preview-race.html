Aasimar
Medium humanoid (planar descent)
Size Medium
Speed 30 ft.
Darkvision 60 ft.
Languages Common, Celestial
Ability Score Increase Charisma +2
Damage Resistances Necrotic, Radiant
Racial Traits:
  • Celestial Resistance: You have resistance to necrotic and radiant damage.
  • Healing Hands: As an action, you can touch a creature and expend a spell slot to restore hit points equal to the spell slot level × 5, or cause a disease or poison affecting it to end.
  • Light Bearer: You know the light cantrip. Charisma is your spellcasting ability for it.
Subrace
Protector Aasimar: Wisdom +1, Radiant Soul (once per long rest, you can activate radiant power for 1 minute, emitting bright light 10 feet and shedding dim light 10 feet further, and gaining the ability to deal radiant damage).
