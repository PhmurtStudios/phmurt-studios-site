Bounty Hunter
A hunter who tracks marks for coin and justice
Skill Proficiencies Investigation, Survival
Tool Proficiencies Thieves' tools
Languages 1 of your choice
Equipment Common clothes, manacles, belt pouch (10 gp)
Feature: Ear to the Ground

You are in frequent contact with people in the segment of society that your chosen quarries move through. These people are willing to provide information freely, and shelter you if authorities pursue you. This information network operates in the major cities of the region you're from, but doesn't extend to other regions.

Personality Traits
  • I'm always calm, no matter what the situation.
  • I laugh loudly and often at the misfortune of others.
  • I'm haunted by memories of my greatest failure, which I relive in my dreams.
Ideals
  • Justice. Marks must face the law for their crimes.
  • Greed. Coin is the only motivator that matters in this world.
Bonds
  • I have a nemesis who escaped my grasp and for whom I'll sacrifice almost anything.
  • Someone I care about was wronged by someone I'm still hunting.
Flaws
  • I'm sloppy when it comes to paying my debts.
  • I mistake poor planning for boldness.
